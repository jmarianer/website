Joey's portfolio

Brier town homepage
As part of my DesignLab course, I reimagined the homepage for my town with a more modern look. I have made available a wireframe, some intermediate versions, and the final design, and will soon make available a fully-functional HTML version.
Collaborative crossword app
I made this app to help my family solve crosswords collaboratively across multiple timezones. Since there is only one user (myself) who creates crosswords, the create workflow is not as nice as it could be. The solving page is used by multiple people (where "multiple" is defined as "five"), so it is somewhat nicer.
Bricks puzzle solver
As an exercise in functional programming, I wrote a solver for Brickscape. The solver is written in Elm and compiled to JavaScript. An upcoming edition of the solver will include a server, written in Haskell, which will decrease solve times and also avoid running on the UI thread. Link to solver.
